Chocolate Chip Recipe

INGREDIENTS

  • Butter -> 250g
  • White Sugar -> 1 cup
  • Brown sugar -> 1 cup
  • Flour -> 3 cup
  • Sea Salt -> 5g
  • Chocolate Chips ->1 cup
  • egg -> 3

SUBSTITUTIONS

Beat the eggs.
Add brown sugar, white sugar and sea salt and beat again.
Add oil and mix. Finally, add the flour and chocolate.

let it cool down.
